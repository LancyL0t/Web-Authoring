My Top 10 Favorite Movies

Interstellar

A sci-fi epic about a team of astronauts traveling through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet as Earth faces an environmental crisis.

Green Mile

A drama about a death row corrections officer who befriends an inmate with mysterious healing abilities, questioning justice and humanity.

Get Out

A psychological horror film where a Black man uncovers a chilling secret while visiting his white girlfriend’s family.

3 Idiots

A comedy-drama about three college friends navigating the pressures of India's rigid education system while pursuing their dreams.

Walle-E

A Pixar animated film about a lonely waste-collecting robot who embarks on a space adventure to restore humanity’s connection to Earth.

Kingsman: The Golden Circle

A spy-action film where the elite Kingsman agents join forces with their American counterparts to stop a global threat.

UP

A heartwarming adventure about an elderly widower and a young boy who travel to South America in a floating house carried by balloons.

Black Panther

A Marvel superhero film about T’Challa, the king of Wakanda, who must defend his kingdom from internal and external threats.

School of Rock

A comedy about a failed musician who poses as a substitute teacher and transforms a class of students into a rock band.

The Matrix

A sci-fi classic where a hacker, Neo, discovers the truth about reality and joins a rebellion against a powerful AI controlling humanity.
